How to Keep Your
Android App Secure

88% of Android Apps use cryptography suboptimally, leaving them insecure. Use this page to learn how to avoid common pitfalls and keep your users safe.

General Advice:

  • Review this guide and make sure you're doing everything correctly
  • When in doubt, do more research
  • Don't try to implement anything yourself. In almost every case it is better to use established libraries and APIs
  • Research every library you use, EVEN NON-CRYPTO ONES.
  • Use CRYPTOLINT to analyze your program for security holes
